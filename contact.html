Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms do you support? +
We support all major platforms including Zoom, Microsoft Teams, WebEx, and custom streaming solutions. Our platform integrates seamlessly with your existing tools.
How many attendees can we support? +
Our platform can handle events with unlimited attendees. We've successfully hosted events with 50,000+ concurrent viewers. Capacity depends on your plan and streaming infrastructure.
What support do you provide? +
We offer email support for all plans, phone support for Professional and Enterprise plans, and 24/7 dedicated support for Enterprise customers. Our average response time is under 2 hours.
Do you offer recordings? +
Yes, all events are automatically recorded in HD quality. Recordings are available immediately after the event and can be shared with attendees or kept private.
What about data security? +
We use enterprise-grade encryption, SOC 2 compliance, and GDPR adherence. All data is stored securely with automatic backups and disaster recovery protocols.
